## Early teachers
The supreme goddess of the pashcimAMnAya system with her a~nga devatA-s as said to have been taught by kubjikAmatam and manthAnabhairavam tradition holds that prior to matsyendra there was the original transmission of the pashchimAMnAya by the god kumAra manifesting as the teach ShaShThIsha and his wife the goddess ShaShThI manifesting as bhagamAlA.  
Their successors were the 9 siddha-s: gaganAnandanAtha, kumuda.a.n
, padma.a.n, deva.a.n, bhairava.a.n, kamala.a.n, shiva.a.n, rAma.a.n, kR^iShNa.a.n. These ancient teachers are offered tarpaNa as part of the opening ritual. 

Now there is a historical tidbit preserved that shivAnandanAtha was originally a saiddhAntika shaiva dhArashiva before his pashcimAMnAya dIkSha. 

## shUdra vaishya lineages
In any case all these are V1s. There are other lineages of shUdra and vaiShya teachers. Like a vaiShya from the andhra country who sells pearls near shrIshaila & his wife shivAmbA and shUdra sahila who makes idols & his wife rAmAmbA from kAshmIra.

The source of these lineages was the atharvan V1 govinda and his wife surachakrAmbA who were the masters of all mysteries and teachers of the above from a village called madhutA in nandApura. 

Then there is the lineage of the kApAlika somasiddhAntin devapAla and his wife ratnadevI. 

## Women
The pashcimAMnAya is quite notable for drawing teachers from throughout greater India and all 4 varNa-s &also charismatic female teachers like shUdra woman indramaNidevI from sopAra (near modern Vasai in Maharashtra) who taught independently of her husband & became 1 of the highest of the 16 siddhA-s of the system. She probably established the original vajreshvarI temple in the vi.  

Another woman who entered the top 16 was vimalA a vaishyA woman who is said to have demolished the nAstika-s in debates at ThaNako~NkaNa (near modern Thane) who were said to flee
there after whenever they heard her Damaru. She is said to have had most number of students of all the 16 top pashcimAMnAya siddha-s and thus initiated several lineages of kubjikA and pratya~NgirA.

## siddha mastery
High siddha of the pashcimAMnAya attains mastery of the following mantrAstra: the pi~NgalAstra, aghorAstra, brahma-khaDga, chaNDikAstra and the ko~NkaNAstra which is supported by the preceding 4. A prayoga of such is last said to have been done for pr^ithivinArAyaNa-shaha-deva.

The siddha & dutI-s also know the octogon of pratya~NgirA, the secret choma-s which they display to each other; only on their successful meeting and matching of chomma can the prayoga meet any success. The li~Nga-s are also installed by a prayoga distinct from saiddhAntika method.
