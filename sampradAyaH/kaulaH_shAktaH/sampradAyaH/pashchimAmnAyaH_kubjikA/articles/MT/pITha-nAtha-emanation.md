title = "pITha nAtha emanation"
+++

The emanation of the 4 pITha-nAtha-s in the maNDala of pashchimAmnAya (the primordial siddhaugha), their nature and meditation on kubjikA conjoined with mitreshAna:

ओ-जा-पू-का  
यस् त्व् अटेच् चैव त्रैलोक्ये  
चिन्ताधारो महाप्रभुः ।  
कामरूपांश-धारी च  
चञ्चलानन्द उच्यते ॥

The one who roams about in the triple-world, bearing the thoughts, the great lord,  
bearing kAmarUpa as his integral part, he is known as cha~nchalAnanda.

अणुरुपे स्थितो यस्मात्  
पर्यटेत् सर्व-जन्तुषु ।
जालपीठे स्वाभावस् तु  
आणवानन्द उच्यते ॥

He who is situated in the form of the atom, roaming around in all organisms,  
of the nature of the jAlandhara-pITha, he is known as ANavAnanda.

समस्तेषु च भूतेषु  
व्यापारं कुरुते तु यः ।
पूर्णपीठांश-भावैश् च  
व्यापारीशः स उच्यते ॥

He who performs the business among all the beings  
has pUrNagiri as his integral part, and is known as vyApArIsha.

आज्ञा-शब्दार्थ-शास्त्राणि  
यत्-सकाशात् प्रवर्तते ।
ओड्डपीटांशभवैश् च  
शिवानन्दः स उच्यते ॥

He from whom the instructions, the meanings of words and scriptures come forth  
has ODDiyAna as his integral part and is known as shivAnanda.

शक्ति-सामार्थ्यतो बुद्धिर्  
आज्ञा यस्य अखण्डिता ।  
त्वं च वै कुब्जिके स्वामि-  
चतुष्-पीठाधिकारिणी ।
मित्रेशान-समायुक्ता  
भावितासि कलेवारे ॥

One whose intellect and command are unbroken due to the shakti,  
that indeed is you O kubjikA, mistress with authority over the 4 pITha-s,  
united with mitreshAna you are mediated upon in the body.
